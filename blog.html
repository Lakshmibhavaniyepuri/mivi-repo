About

Mivi is a homegrown Indian electronics brand that specializes in designing and manufacturing high-quality audio and mobile accessories. Founded in 2015 by Viswanadh Kandula and Midhula Devabhaktuni, Mivi has quickly become a popular name for affordable and stylish tech products, including Bluetooth earphones, wireless speakers, chargers, and cables.

Career

Careers at Mivi are built around innovation, creativity, and a strong "Make in India" ethos. As a fast-growing brand in the consumer electronics space, Mivi offers exciting career opportunities in engineering, design, marketing, manufacturing, and customer support.

Mivi encourages a dynamic and youthful work environment, where ideas are welcomed and individual contributions are recognized.

Why work at Mivi?

  • 100% Made in India initiative
  • Startup culture with strong values
  • Learning and development opportunities
  • Inclusive and collaborative team
  • Focus on innovation and quality

Education

Viswanadh Kandula and Midhula Devabhaktuni, the co-founders of Mivi, bring a blend of strong educational backgrounds and global experience to the brand.

Viswanadh earned a degree in Electronics Engineering in the U.S., and Midhula studied Marketing there as well. Their education plays a key role in Mivi’s design and market strategies.
